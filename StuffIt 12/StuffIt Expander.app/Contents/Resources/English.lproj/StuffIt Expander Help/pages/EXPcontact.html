Getting Technical Support

If you're having a technical problem, and you can't find the answer in the included Help files and documentation, please go to our comprehensive Technical Support web site, click the " Knowledge Base" link and follow the on screen instructions.

In the event that you look for an answer but do not find it, you will be given the opportunity to submit a Technical Support Request to our Support Team where it will be addressed online by the Support Specialists. If you do not have a user id or password for the system just click on the login assistance and register your product or go to Product Registration

Help Us Help You

When submitting questions to our Technical Support Forum, we need as much of the following information as possible:

  • The exact version of the product you are using - This can be found in the product "About box" (available in the product menu (just to the right of the Apple menu when your product is in the foreground of your screen).
  • The exact version of the Mac OS you are using - This can be found by choosing "About this Mac" from the Apple menu in the Finder on Mac OS X.
  • A step by step description of how to reproduce the issue
  • The exact text of any Error messages
